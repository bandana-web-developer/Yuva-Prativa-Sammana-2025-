ARGUS YUVA PRATIVA SAMMANA 2025

Argus News is organising Yuva Prativa Sammana, the Youth Excellence Awards, an initiative to celebrate the Leaders and Changemakers of tomorrow. The Awards are a step towards recognition of the exceptional contributions of Odisha's youth working for the nationâ€™s development in major fields like Entertainment, Sports, Entrepreneurship, Education, Health, Agriculture, Environment, Technology, Governance, and other vital sectors.
